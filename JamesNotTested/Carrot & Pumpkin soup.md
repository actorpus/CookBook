### Ingredients
- 1 onion
- 150g diced pumpkin
- 150g diced carrot
- 150g diced potato
- 75g butter
- 1 pt vegetable stock
### Method
- saut√© onions in butter until soft
- stir in carrots, pumpkin, and potato and cook for 3-5 minutes
- add stock and season gently
- bring to boil then simmer 25-30 mins
- blitz to a thick soup
