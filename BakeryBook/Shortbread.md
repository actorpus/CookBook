### Ingredients

- 60g plain flour
- 40g soft butter
- 30g caster sugar
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

### Instructions

- crumb flour and butter
- add sugar and vanilla extract
- form a dough, taking care not to overwork it
- roll out onto a floured worksurface to 0.5cm thickness
- bake for 15 mins at 160 fan
